AddTemporaryVisualEffect(GameObject, EffectCondition, bool, string)

Adds a custom TemporaryVisualEffect to all CharacterBodies. Will be updated in the CharacterBody just after vanilla TemporaryVisualEffects. This overload lets you choose between scaling your effect based on RoR2.CharacterBody.radius or RoR2.CharacterBody.bestFitRadius. Returns true if successful.
